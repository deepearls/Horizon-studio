About Us

Horizon Fitness Studio is a fitness community that offers memberships, classes, and personal training in a fun atmosphere. Our approach is to allow Our clients to set the pace and encourage them along the way to meet their fitness goals. Our physical location is located in Ilorin, Kwara State, Nigeria. Howewver, you can reach out to us from anywhere for an online class schedule.
